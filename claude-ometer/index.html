Claude-ometer

You're either using AI at 10% of its capacity and don't know it, or you've built a system and can't explain what level you're at. This tool solves both.

The Problem

Everyone says they're "using AI." But the PM who pastes a question into ChatGPT and the PM who has a structured Claude workspace with persistent memory, custom skills, and automated workflows are both "using AI." The distance between them is enormous — and neither can measure it.

Without a scoring model, you can't diagnose what you're missing, you can't track improvement, and you can't explain the gap to your team. "I use AI better" isn't actionable. "I'm at Level 3, here's what Level 5 requires" is.

What This Will Be

A diagnostic scoring model that measures AI-native PM maturity across dimensions:

  • Context depth: Does your AI start from zero every conversation, or does it have persistent product context?
  • Skill sophistication: Are you prompting ad-hoc, or running structured skills that encode your best thinking?
  • Workflow coverage: How much of your PM workflow has AI integrated — discovery, analysis, communication, planning?
  • Output quality: Does AI output require heavy editing, or does it produce near-final work?
  • Feedback loops: Does your AI system improve over time, or does it plateau at the same quality?

Self-assessment + improvement roadmap. Score yourself, see the gap, know exactly what to build next.
